Who We Are

Family-Owned, Craft-Focused

Divine Decks was founded in 2010 by Marcus and Linda Reeves, two people with a simple belief: your backyard should be an extension of your home, not an afterthought.

What started as a small two-person operation has grown into a trusted team of skilled craftsmen, designers, and project managers — all united by the same passion for quality outdoor living.

Every deck we build carries our family name, so we treat every project as if it were our own home. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just beautiful, lasting workmanship.
