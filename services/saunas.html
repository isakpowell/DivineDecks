Our Services

Saunas

Custom outdoor saunas built with premium timber and quality heating systems — a genuine luxury for your Sydney backyard.

— What We Do

We design and build custom outdoor saunas to suit your backyard, your budget, and your lifestyle. Whether you're after a compact barrel sauna at the edge of the garden or a full-sized cedar cabin integrated into an existing deck, we handle the entire build — from site preparation and structural framing through to lining, bench construction, and heater installation.

— Materials and Heating

We work primarily with Western Red Cedar and Nordic Spruce — both traditional sauna timbers chosen for their low thermal conductivity, resistance to moisture, and the pleasant aroma they release under heat. For heating we offer both traditional wood-fired and modern electric options, including infrared units for those who prefer lower operating temperatures and faster warm-up times.

— Built for Sydney

Outdoor saunas thrive in Sydney's climate — cool winter evenings make them genuinely useful year-round, and the outdoor lifestyle of Inner West and eastern suburbs homes means they become a natural extension of how people live. We ensure all builds meet Australian electrical standards and local council requirements.
