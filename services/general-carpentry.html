Our Services

General Carpentry

Skilled carpentry work using premium Australian hardwoods — from decking boards to structural framing and everything in between.

— What We Do

Our general carpentry service covers the full range of structural and finish timber work that goes into an exceptional outdoor space. Whether it's framing, joinery, pergola beams, screening, or bespoke built-in features, our tradesmen bring the same level of care and precision to every job — big or small.

— Australian Hardwoods

We work primarily with Australian native hardwoods — Spotted Gum, Blackbutt, and Merbau — chosen for their natural resistance to Sydney's climate, their beauty, and their longevity. These species are significantly harder and more durable than imported softwoods, meaning your investment lasts decades with minimal upkeep.

— Attention to Detail

Good carpentry is invisible — it's the joints that fit perfectly, the lines that run true, and the surfaces that feel solid underfoot. Our team has decades of combined experience working on Inner West and Sydney homes, and we take pride in work that stands up to scrutiny long after the job is done.
